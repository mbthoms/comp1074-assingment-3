Welcome to Indy Graphics

For over 25 years Indy Graphics has been providing print products to customers from our local area to the four corners of the world. We bring experience from many types of printing to make sure you receive the service, quality and price that you require. Our vast range of products allow us to be the right choice for your project large or small.
We have partnered with other manufacturers to bring all of your print needs under one roof.

No more dealing with multiple suppliers wasting time and energy, just one number to call and one company to bring it all together.
From basic labels & decals with and without CSA certification, Lamicoids and forms to subsurface printing with specialty 3M adhesives for powder coat or rough textured surfaces we can handle it all.

About

Over 25 years ago Indy Graphics was started with a vision to provide top quality print products all in one place. The largest complaint heard in the market was having to source from so many different companies for all the different print needs.

Indy Graphics identified the problem and created a one source approach for print needs, from labels, decals, forms, brochures to metal plates, lamacoids and even promotional products.

We have partnered with top quality trade manufactures to provide anything that Indy Graphics does not complete in house. Our volumes purchased over each year allow us to provide quality, price and service to our clients that makes your purchasing easier.
